Our story

A salon on
Rathgar Road

Thomas Collins Hair Studio sits at 101 Rathgar Road in Dublin 6. It’s a small, friendly salon where you tend to see the same face each visit and never feel rushed out the door.

Everyday cuts and colour, highlights, balayage, blow-dries and treatments. We take the time to get it right, and we’d love to look after your hair.
