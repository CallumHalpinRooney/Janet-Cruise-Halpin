About the artist

Rooted in nature, drawn by hand.

Orla Giury is an independent artist working from the Irish coast, drawing intricate Celtic and free-flowing line art entirely by hand. Sea, sky and growing things find their way in — each piece begins as a single line and grows, loop by loop, into trees of life, blossoms and knotwork.

Most begin as commissions. Somewhere in the pattern there's usually something personal — a name, a date, a few words — woven in so quietly that only the person it's for would know to look. A piece made to hold onto someone, or a moment worth keeping.

What starts on paper finds its way onto prints, cards and keepsakes, so a little bit of hand-drawn Irish art — and its meaning — can be kept close.
