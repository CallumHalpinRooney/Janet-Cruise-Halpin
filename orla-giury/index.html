About the artist

A small Irish studio, and a lot of tiny lines.

Orla Giury is an independent artist working from Ireland, drawing intricate Celtic and free-flowing line art entirely by hand. Each piece begins as a single line and grows — loop by loop — into trees of life, hearts, swans, stags and knotwork.

What starts on paper finds its way onto prints, greeting cards, mugs, tumblers and seasonal pieces, so a little bit of hand-drawn Irish art can live in everyday things.

This site is simply the front door — the work itself lives and sells across her socials and shops.
