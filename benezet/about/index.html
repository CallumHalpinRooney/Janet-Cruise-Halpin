The gallery

Bought on knowledge. Sold on trust.

Benezet is a private dealer in fine antiques and objets d'art, working quietly out of Dublin for those who care about the object in front of them.

The business has one habit that hasn't changed in decades: buy only what you understand, and describe it honestly. Every piece here has been handled, researched and vetted before it earned a place in the collection. Where a thing has been restored, we say so. Where a mark is worn or a plate has foxed with age, we say that too. Nothing is dressed up to be more than it is.

That plainness is deliberate. A good antique doesn't need selling — it needs the right person to see it clearly. So there are no prices online and no baskets to fill. Each piece is offered price on application, because value in this trade is a conversation, not a sticker.

When something catches your eye, enquire. We'll tell you what we know — the period, the maker where it can be attributed, the provenance, the condition — and arrange for you to see it in person or collect it when you're ready. That's the whole business: honest objects, honestly described, handed over to people who'll keep them well.
