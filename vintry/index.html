The House

A merchant's eye,
a neighbour's welcome.

The Vintry has poured itself into the corner of Rathgar Road for a generation — long enough to know our regulars' tastes better than they do, and to have laid down the bottles worth waiting for.

We buy the way we drink: led by curiosity, never by fashion. Classed-growth Bordeaux and grower Champagne sit happily beside a €17 Marlborough that overdelivers every single time. If it's on our shelf, it earned its place.

Step in for a Tuesday-night red or a cellar-defining magnum — you'll get the same considered word from the same people who chose it.

“We don't stock labels. We stock wines we'd be glad to open ourselves.” — The Vintry, Rathgar
