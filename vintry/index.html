Discover

A world for wine lovers, everywhere.

The Vintry was established in 1991. Ever since, we've poured ourselves into our award-winning shop in Rathgar, Dublin 6 — and, these days, online too.

We carry a full range of fine wines, right back to older vintages, alongside an extensive selection of spirits, liqueurs, cocktails and craft beers — many you simply won't find on a supermarket shelf.

We also offer a complete party service and a range of gift boxes, including bespoke, customised gift cases built to suit every requirement and every budget.

About Us

A merchant's eye,
a neighbour's welcome.

Wine should be a pleasure, never a test. Our team trains continually in matching wine with food, and through our own education arm we taste through the range together every week — so the advice you get is lived-in, not learned by rote.

Between us we hold qualifications from the Wine & Spirit Education Trust, the wine industry's recognised training body: four Certificates, four Higher Certificates, two Distinctions and two Diplomas.

But knowledge only counts if it's useful. We couple it with a warm, down-to-earth approach that starts with you — your taste, your occasion, your budget — and finds the bottle that fits.
