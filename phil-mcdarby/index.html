Capturing a sense of magic and wonder.

A digital artist and photographer working in two worlds — high-fantasy art of dragons and faery woods, and fine-art photographs of the Irish landscape. Original prints of both, made to order.

The Golden Wood — photography

The work

At the heart of the work is a desire to capture a sense of magic and wonder — that feeling of child-like excitement and discovery that we can lose touch with as adults.

Irish woodland, photographed and then painted into — moss, water and ancient trees given over to dragons and quiet spirits. A world just beyond our sight.
