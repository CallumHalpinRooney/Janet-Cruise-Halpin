About the artist

A bit about Orla.

Orla Guiry is an independent artist from Ireland. She draws intricate Celtic and free-flowing line work, all by hand. Every piece starts as a single line and builds up into trees of life, blossoms, animals and knotwork.

Most pieces are commissions. There's usually something personal hidden in the pattern: a name, a date, a few words, worked in so quietly that only the person it's for would know to look. Often it's a way to remember someone, or to mark a moment worth keeping.

The work also goes onto prints, cards and keepsakes, so a piece can be kept close.
